About Zed Marketplace

Zed Marketplace is Zambia's premier online platform connecting buyers and sellers across the country. Our mission is to make e-commerce accessible, safe, and convenient for everyone. Whether you're looking for electronics, fashion, home essentials, or services, we provide a trusted space for Zambians to buy and sell with confidence.

We empower local sellers by giving them an easy-to-use platform to showcase their products, reach more customers, and grow their businesses. For buyers, we offer a seamless shopping experience with reliable delivery and secure transactions.
