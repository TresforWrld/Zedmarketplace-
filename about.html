About Zed Marketplace

Zed Marketplace is Zambia’s trusted online shopping and selling platform, Created by Tresfor Zulu under the ANICADE ELEVATE program. built to connect local buyers and sellers in one secure digital space.

Our mission is to empower and connect Zambian entrepreneurs, small businesses, and independent sellers by giving them access to a modern e-commerce platform without barriers.

We believe in simplicity, transparency, and growth — for sellers and buyers alike.
